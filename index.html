FAQ

Common Questions

Will AI replace my team?
No — and that's the whole point. AI is a force multiplier. I help your team use AI to handle repetitive work so they can focus on creative, strategic, and high-value tasks. People who learn to work with AI become more valuable, not less.
What kinds of businesses do you work with?
Any business ready to embrace AI — from startups building AI-first products to established companies modernising their operations. I've worked across SaaS, professional services, e-commerce, and more.
What AI tools and platforms do you work with?
I'm tool-agnostic and pick what's best for your situation — Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot, open-source models, automation platforms (Make, n8n, Zapier), custom-built agents, and more. The goal is the right solution, not a specific vendor.
Do you write code or just advise?
Both. I can advise your team strategically, coach them hands-on, and write production code — AI integrations, agent systems, automations, full-stack applications. Whatever the engagement needs.
